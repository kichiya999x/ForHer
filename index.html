To my favorite person,


I am so lucky to have someone like you.

You are the good in my mornings, afternoons, and nights.

You are every beautiful word I could possibly think of.

I love everything about you.


You are the person I want to cry with when things are bad,

and celebrate with when things are good.

You deserve all the good things,

and I want to love you in ways that you deserve.


We may live far away from each other,

but you are close to my heart.


I love you.
